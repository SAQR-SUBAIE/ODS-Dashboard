Moderation : back

/ban


bans a member.

/kick


kicks a member.

/lock


disables @everyone from sending messages in specific channel.

/move


moves a member to another voice channel.

/unlock


removes denied sending messages from @everyone in specific channel.

/imprisonment


Imprisoning someone and preventing them from seeing the channels

/unimprisonment


unimprisonment a member.

/mute


mute a member from text channels so they cannot type.

/unmute


unmutes a member.

/role


add/remove role(s) for a member.

/purge


cleans messages from a channel.

/roles


Get a list of server roles and member counts.

/temprole


Assign / NO Assign a role for limited time that persists if the user leaves and joins.
